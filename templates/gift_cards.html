Purchasing In-Store

Know someone who really needs to be properly fitted for a pair of shoes? Give them Fitgeek gift card -- their feet will thank you!

Gift cards are available in any denomination. Come in to the store to pick up your gift card, or call us at 408-418-5888 to purchase a gift card over the phone and we will mail it to any address you like!

Don't have time (or can't) to come to our store? No problem! Fill out the form below to begin purchasing a gift certificate from the comfort of your own home!

A gift certificate will be emailed to the recipient after your order has been paid for, and a receipt will be emailed to you. If you would like to give the gift certificate to the recipient yourself, simply put your own email as the Recipient's Email and the certificate will be emailed to you.
