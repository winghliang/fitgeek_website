About us

  • Home
  • About us

Fitgeek Sports is a local, family-owned specialty running and walking store located in Cupertino, California, in the heart of Silicon Valley. As a small business, we take pride in providing the best customer service to each and every one of our customers and supporting the local community and economy.

We proudly serve the entire Silicon Valley Bay Area, including Cupertino, Sunnyvale, San Jose, Mountain View, Santa Clara, Saratoga, Los Altos, Palo Alto, Los Gatos, Fremont, Milpitas, Campbell and much more!

We established Fitgeek to promote passion for living an active lifestyle. We believe it's the simple things in life like good health and good friends that make us happy.

Our goal is to help you find the running and walking gear that is best for you. We offer a comprehensive shoe-fitting process to help you find the right pair of shoes for your particular foot type. Our shoe-fitting process includes a custom gait analysis, measurements of your foot, and use of state-of the art video equipment to break down your stride frame-by-frame. We also offer top-of-the-line running and walking apparel, accessories, and nutrition products.

Through our knowledgeable and friendly staff, we offer an alternative to the "big box" stores, websites, and mall locations. Whether you're just getting into running or walking, training for your first race, or a seasoned marathoner, we have what you need to perform at your best.
